Recreationals

Obstacle Course Racing:

A personal challenge that I picked up to encourage myself to get more active and fit. It is not only a physical challenge (i.e. a 10ft wall, 100lb weight,etc.), but the mental challenge of convincing yourself that you can complete the race itself.

Paintball:

Starting roughly 5 years ago I began playing at Friendly Fire Paintball in Upton,MA. From there I have gone on to play at massive games at Albrightsville, PA at Skirmish Paintball's "Invasion of Normandy" event. I love the sport dearly and also pursue it to this day with my mother and father accompanying me on the battlefield.

Bowling:

A hobby that I picked up during my high school years that I actually had a talent for. We played regionally as well as against other teams across the state. I still play recreationally to this day.

LARP:

Live Action Role Play or LARP is an activity /event that I had picked up around March 2018. It is an outlet of escape in which I take on a fake persona to practice my improvisation skills as well as have some genuine fun screaming about nothing of importance.
